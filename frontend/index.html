Our Story

A little corner, a lot of care

Cup O'Aroma started as a Sunday ritual between friends. We wanted a place that felt like a soft sweater — calm, warm, and easy to stay in. Today we still roast in small batches and bake before the city wakes.

  • 🌿 Locally roasted, single-origin beans
  • 🥚 Pastries made with cage-free, local eggs
  • ♻️ Compostable cups & zero-waste goals
